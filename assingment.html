Severe floods in Uttarkashi, life comes to a standstill

Severe Floods in Uttarkashi: 5 Dead, Up to 100 Missing Heavy rainfall and a sudden cloudburst have caused devastating floods in Uttarkashi district of Uttarakhand. The Bhagirathi River and several local rivers are overflowing, submerging multiple villages and cutting off road connectivity. So far, 5 deaths have been confirmed, while between 50 and 100 people are reported missing. NDRF, SDRF, and Army personnel are carrying out continuous rescue and relief operations. Several bridges have been washed away, and some buildings have been reduced to rubble. The Meteorological Department has issued a warning for more rainfall over the next 48 hours.
